Often asking questions on pictures web store

What kinds of pictures do you have?

On the site there are paintings from both the recognized masters and reproductions of the famous paintings.

What are the sizes of pictures?

Paintings of artists already have the standard size specified in the description.
Reproductions are of several sizes. If you did not find the size you need, you can order it from us. Please contact us following this link.

Do I need to prepay?

NO! You pay only after you have received and inspected the picture.

Can I order a frame for my painting?

Yes, of course, you can buy a painting with or without a frame (See the picture frames). All the pictures by default are made on the stretcher with a gallery stretch, which, in fact, looks very nice and is suitable for any interior.

Can I take the painting back?

Yes, of course, you can return the picture according to all the current laws of the country on the protection of consumers' rights.
